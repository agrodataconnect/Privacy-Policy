Privacy Policy

AgroData Connect

Last updated: March 15, 2026

AgroData Connect is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users, partners, farmers, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information through our website, digital platforms, and integrations with services such as Meta and WhatsApp APIs.

1. Who We Are

AgroData Connect is an AgTech initiative based in Colombia that develops AI-powered tools and data platforms to help farmers make better decisions, improve productivity, and reduce agricultural losses.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, email address, and organization
  • Information submitted through contact forms or partnership requests
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Agricultural or project-related data voluntarily shared by users

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our digital tools and services
  • Respond to inquiries and partnership requests
  • Share updates about programs, research, or opportunities
  • Generate aggregated insights for agricultural innovation and sustainability

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted partners, research institutions, or service providers that help operate or improve our services. Data is shared only when necessary and with appropriate safeguards.

5. International Data Processing

Because AgroData Connect collaborates with international partners and organizations, some data may be processed or stored outside Colombia. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect personal information.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Data Deletion Instructions

If you want your personal data deleted from AgroData Connect systems, you may submit a request by email.

Email: agrodataconnect@gmail.com

Please include your name, contact information, and a brief description of your request. We will review and process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
