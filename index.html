Privacy Policy

AgroData Connect

Last updated: March 15, 2026

AgroData Connect is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users, partners, farmers, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information through our website and digital services.

1. Who We Are

AgroData Connect is an AgTech company based in Colombia that develops AI-powered tools and data platforms to help farmers make better decisions, improve productivity, and reduce agricultural losses.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and organization
  • Information submitted through contact forms
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Agricultural or project-related data voluntarily shared by users

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve our digital tools and services
  • Respond to inquiries and partnership requests
  • Share updates about programs, research, or opportunities
  • Generate aggregated insights for agricultural innovation

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted partners, research institutions, or service providers that support the operation of our platform. Data is shared only when necessary and with appropriate safeguards.

5. International Data Processing

Because AgroData Connect collaborates with international partners, some data may be processed outside Colombia. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect personal information.

6. User Rights

Users may request to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Data Deletion Instructions

If you want your data deleted from AgroData Connect systems, please send a request to:

agrodataconnect@gmail.com

Include your name, contact information, and a brief description of your request. We will process the request within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.
